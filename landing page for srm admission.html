About SRM College

SRM College is one of the top institutions offering a range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs. Our mission is to provide world-class education, foster innovation, and prepare students for success.

SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST), commonly known as SRM University, offers a diverse array of undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and doctoral programs across various disciplines. Below is an overview of the courses available at SRMIST's main campus in Kattankulathur, Chennai:

Undergraduate Programs

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech):
Specializations include Aerospace Engineering, Automobile Engineering, Biotechnology, Chemical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, among others.
Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch): A comprehensive program focusing on architectural design and planning.
Bachelor of Design (B.Des) in Interior Design: Emphasizes creative and technical aspects of interior spaces.
Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA): Covers various facets of business management and administration.
Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com): Focuses on commerce, finance, and accounting principles.
Bachelor of Science (B.Sc): Available in disciplines such as Biotechnology, Computer Science, Visual Communication, and more.
Bachelor of Arts (BA): Offered in subjects like Journalism and Mass Communication, English, etc.
Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA): Focuses on computer applications and software development.

Postgraduate Programs:

Master of Technology (M.Tech): Specializations include Computer Science and Engineering, VLSI Design, Power Systems, and more.
Master of Business Administration (MBA): Provides advanced knowledge in business strategies, management practices, and leadership skills.
Master of Science (M.Sc): Offered in fields like Biotechnology, Physics, Chemistry, and others.
Master of Arts (MA): Available in English, Journalism and Mass Communication, etc.
Master of Computer Applications (MCA): Focuses on advanced computer application and software development.
Diploma and Certificate Programs:
Diploma in Hotel Management and Catering Science:
A three-year program preparing students for the hospitality industry.
Diploma in Yoga: A one-year program focusing on the principles and practices of yoga.
General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM): A three-year diploma in nursing.

Doctoral Programs:

Ph.D.: Offered in various disciplines such as Engineering, Management, Science, and Humanities.
